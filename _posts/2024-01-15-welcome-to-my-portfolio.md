## Why TeXt Theme?

I chose the TeXt theme for this portfolio because:

1. **Clean Design**: The minimalist aesthetic keeps the focus on content
2. **Responsive**: Works beautifully on all devices
3. **Customizable**: Easy to adapt to my personal brand
4. **Feature-Rich**: Includes everything needed for a professional portfolio
5. **Well-Documented**: Great documentation makes setup and customization straightforward
